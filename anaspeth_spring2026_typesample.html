Harbor Seal

A Type Sample with Orpheus Pro and Source Serif.

Harbor Seal by Tigerspring11.

A dollop of frolic restrained

Head hanging down into the sea

Like a raindrop on a pane

Waiting the pull of gravity.

   

Are you testing the waters

With nose instead of toe

Will you drop to swim or fish

Are you just not ready to go?

   

Your kin remain napping

Strewn languidly on the rocks

Will they follow you into the sea

or snort and snooze and let you be...
